Use case: Content summary

Description
Isabel is the head of a group of journalists specialized in new trends. Her group has to collect, prepare and film every day to keep up with the pace. So there's no question of wasting time reading long articles or watching hours of streaming videos. That is why she decided to initiate the creation of a service. On the one hand, it offers summaries of these resources and alerts a journalist if interesting topics are detected. On the other hand, it would expose them, in long and summarized form, to other colleagues for further investigation.
Requirements
  • The client needs to convey summary constraint with options(length: by words or paragraphs, language: formal or informal).
  • The client needs to express the topics of interest that should be comprised in the summary.
  • The server needs to summarize the resources, for example, shorten a 10-page report to one page of points.
Solutions
  • HTTP header Accept: text/summarised or text/turtle+summarised
Content negotiation dimensions
  • Profile? link to vocabulary if we want the summary to contain a vocabulary
Other use cases
  • Summarization/Characterization of datasets.

Use case: Entailment regime

Description
Lucy is the manager of a company that is a vendor of a SPARQL engine. Their call center is constantly being asked what entailment regime they support and how that ER is configured and enabled.
She wants to address this issue and provide a way to query the data without needing to ask the vendor, and negotiate RDF graphs with inferred triples according to a specific entailment regime.
Requirements
  • The client needs to convey the entailment regime.
  • The server needs to convey a response with the requested entailment regime or send an error message.
Solutions
  • Negotiation by profile as SHACL documents, use the sh:entailment with IRI indication [L1].

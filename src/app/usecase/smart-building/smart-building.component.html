Use case: Smart building

Description
EMSE is trying to make the Fayol building smart by integrating IOT sensors to gather humidity, presence, heat data. Users want to be able to request the data about a resource in this case a room. The requested data could be either real time, a past time or aggregated on a time lapse. One concern is that the collected data could invade privacy of the office workers.
Requirements
  • Serve different versions of freshness of the resource.
  • Anonymize the response to keep the privacy.
